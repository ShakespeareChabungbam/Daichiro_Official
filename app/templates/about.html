Who We Are

Daichiro Professional
Skills Academy

Daichiro Professional Skills Academy is a holistic development centre committed to nurturing confident, disciplined, and future-ready young individuals. We believe education should go beyond textbook; building communication skills, character strength, leadership qualities, and emotional balance.

Through structured programs like SkillNest After Club, we provide homework support alongside life skills training, value education, authentic games, and digital discipline awareness in a safe and guided environment.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academics and real-life readiness, while supporting parents in raising responsible, confident, and compassionate children.

"At Daichiro, we don't just teach skills — We shape character."

— Sashipriya Potsangbam, Founder
