Hello and welcome!

This is your complete guide to operating the DAICHIRO website. This 
manual has been written so that even someone with zero technology 
experience can understand how every part of this platform works.

HOW TO READ THIS MANUAL:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Think of this website like a modern office building with different 
floors. Each floor has a specific purpose and only certain people 
can enter. This manual will walk you through every floor, every room, 
and every button you need to know.

You do NOT need to understand code. You do NOT need to call a 
developer for everyday tasks. This manual explains everything in 
plain business language.

WHAT THIS WEBSITE DOES FOR YOUR BUSINESS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Allows parents to apply for SkillNest admissions directly online.
• Lets students take a detailed 5-part career assessment on any device.

  (1) Reads and analyzes each student's digital assessment answers

  (2) Scans handwritten paper assessment forms — if a student fills 
      out a physical paper form instead of using a computer, simply 
      take a photo and upload it. The AI reads the handwriting, 
      extracts all the answers, and saves them digitally for you.
• Gives you total control over pricing, staff access, and all records.
• Protects your business data with daily backups and a Trash Bin 
  so nothing is ever accidentally lost forever.

Imagine your website is a high-end restaurant. Even if you don't 
cook, it helps to understand what kitchen tools are being used so 
you can appreciate why the food (your website) is so exceptional.

Here is what is powering your DAICHIRO platform:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  COMPONENT             TECHNOLOGY USED       WHAT IT DOES       │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  The Engine            Python                Runs all logic     │
│  The Server Framework  Flask + Gunicorn      Handles web traffic│
│  The Database          PostgreSQL            Stores all records │

IMPORTANT NOTE ABOUT AWS EC2:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Your website is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS EC2). While AWS 
is very powerful, you may occasionally see the website go offline or 
temporarily unavailable. Do not panic — this is sometimes a planned 
security reset mechanism built in by the developers to protect your 
system and re-stabilize performance. The website will return online 
automatically.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. HOMEPAGE (daichiro.in)

[PAGE 09]      SECTION 2C: THE AI GEMINI BRAIN (Career Reports)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This is the most powerful and unique feature of your platform. 
The DAICHIRO website is connected directly to Google's most 
advanced Artificial Intelligence: Gemini.

WHAT THE AI DOES:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
After a student completes their 5-part career assessment, the AI 
reads all of their answers — their interests, their academic 
aptitude scores, their personality traits, and their career vision 
— and then generates a completely personalized report for them.

The AI has been given a special professional identity: it acts as 
a "Senior Professional Psychologist and Career Counselor with 25+ 
years of experience in India, specializing in adolescent guidance."

WHAT THE REPORT INCLUDES:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. PROFILE SUMMARY: A 4-sentence personal summary that directly 
     addresses the student by their own first name and references 
     their specific answers. It is never generic.

  2. CAREER PATHWAYS (3 Options with Fit Percentage):
     For example: "Engineering (78% Fit)" with 3 specific recommended 
     actions. Local North-East India institutions are suggested 
     alongside top pan-India colleges.

  3. PERSONALIZED 5-YEAR ROADMAP: A year-by-year plan from Year 1 
     to Year 5, each with a theme (e.g., "Foundation", "Growth", 
     "Specialization") and 3 specific milestones to achieve.

MANAGING THE AI KEY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. Go to: Gemini AI Settings in your left menu.
  2. To activate the AI, you enter your Google Gemini API Key here.
  3. SECURITY: Before saving, the system asks you to re-confirm 
     your ADMIN password. This prevents anyone from changing 
     this critical setting without your permission.
  4. You can also choose which Gemini model to use:
     • gemini-2.5-flash (Recommended — fast and smart)
     • gemini-2.5-pro (Most powerful, slower)
     • gemini-2.0-flash (Older, stable)

[PAGE 10]         SECTION 2D: THE CAREER ASSESSMENT SYSTEM
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This section is the heart of the DAICHIRO career counseling service. 
Every student completes a structured assessment, and you use the 
AI-generated report to provide professional career guidance.

UNDERSTANDING THE ASSESSMENT STAGES:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Your Assessments page organizes every student record into clear groups:

  🔄 IN PROGRESS   — The student is currently logged in and taking 
                     the assessment right now, or they started and 
                     have not finished yet.

  ⏳ PENDING AI    — The student has finished and submitted their 
                     assessment. The AI has NOT analyzed it yet. 
                     You need to click "Analyze" to start the AI.

  🧠 AI COMPLETE   — The AI has fully analyzed the assessment. 
                     The career report and roadmap are ready for 
                     you to view and discuss with the family.

  🗑️ TRASH BIN    — Assessments you have soft-deleted. They are 
                     safely stored here and can be restored.

HOW TO RUN THE AI ANALYSIS ON A STUDENT:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. Go to "Assessments" in your left menu.
  2. Click on the "Pending AI Analysis" group.
  3. Find the student's assessment card.
  4. Click the "Analyze with AI" button.
  5. Wait a few seconds. The AI reads all 5 sections of the 
     student's answers and generates the full report.
  6. The assessment moves to the "AI Analysis Complete" group.
  7. Click "View Report" to see the full roadmap.

HOW TO PRINT THE REPORT AS A PDF:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. Open a completed assessment (AI Complete group).
  2. Click "Download PDF" or "Print Report."
  3. A clean, professionally formatted PDF is generated that you 
     can print and hand to the family during your consultation.

CREATING A STUDENT ACCOUNT (Client Login Credentials):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. On the Assessments page, click "Create Client."
  2. Optionally, enter the student's name for your own reference.
  3. The system instantly generates a random Username 
     (e.g., student_74293) and a temporary Password.
  4. A confirmation message appears on screen showing these 
     credentials. Your staff writes them down and hands them 
     to the student on paper.




════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
[PAGE 11]       SECTION 2E: SCANNING HANDWRITTEN PAPER FORMS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Some students prefer to fill out their career assessment on a 
physical paper form instead of using a computer. DAICHIRO has a 
solution for this as well — it uses AI to convert handwritten 
papers into digital records instantly.

WHEN WOULD YOU USE THIS?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
When a student fills out the DAICHIRO "Student Career Interest & 
Aptitude Questionnaire" on paper and hands it to you physically.

HOW IT WORKS STEP BY STEP:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  STEP 1: Go to Assessments → click "Upload Assessment / Scan Paper."
  STEP 2: Take a clear photo of the student's paper form using your 
          phone camera or a webcam. Upload the photo(s).
           • You can upload MULTIPLE photos if the form has 2 pages.
           • The system automatically compresses images so they 
             upload quickly even on a slow internet connection.
  STEP 3: The Google Gemini AI "reads" the photo. It looks at:
           • The student's handwritten personal details
           • Which checkboxes were ticked in the Interests section
           • Which circles were filled in the Aptitude rating (1–5)
           • Which personality statements were selected
           • The student's handwritten Career Vision answers
  STEP 4: A "Review Scanned Data" page appears. You can see exactly 
          what the AI extracted from the handwriting. If the AI 
          misread something, you can manually correct it here.
  STEP 5: Click "Save & Analyze." The corrected data is saved into 
          your database AND the AI immediately generates the full 
          career report. The assessment goes straight to 
          "AI Analysis Complete."

NOTE: If you are not satisfied with the AI analysis result, you 
can always click "Re-Analyze" on any assessment at any time to 
run the AI again and get a fresh report.

Your team members (receptionists, teachers, counselors) can each 
have their own secure login. They assist with daily operations 
but cannot access your sensitive business or financial data.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. Your staff go to: daichiro.in/admin/login
     (This is the SAME login page as your Admin login.)
  2. They type their assigned Staff Username and Password.
  3. The system recognizes them as an Employee (not Admin) and

FIRST LOGIN — MANDATORY PASSWORD CHANGE:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The very first time a new staff member logs in, the website will 
freeze their screen and force them to create a new, private 
password before they can do anything else.

The new password must include:
  ✓ At least 8 characters
  ✓ At least one UPPERCASE letter
  ✓ At least one lowercase letter
  ✓ At least one number
  ✓ At least one special character (e.g., ! @ # $)

You do not have to worry about setting this up — the system 
enforces it automatically.

WHAT STAFF CAN DO (Based on Permissions You Set):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  • Appointments — View and manage counseling bookings.
  • Assessments — Help manage student assessment records.
  • SkillNest — View and manage after-school admissions.

The PRIMARY use-case for employees is to quickly generate new 
student login credentials:
  1. Staff clicks "Create Client" in their portal.
  2. The system generates a random Username (e.g., student_82051) 
     and a temporary Password automatically.
  3. Staff writes these down and hands them to the student

CREATING A NEW EMPLOYEE ACCOUNT (Done by Admin):

This is the private portal where students log in to complete their

HOW STUDENTS ACCESS THE EXAM PORTAL:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. The student receives a Username and temporary Password 
     from your receptionist or staff member (on a slip of paper).
  2. They open a browser (on phone, tablet, or laptop) and go to:
     daichiro.in/client/login
  3. They type in their temporary credentials and click "Login."

MANDATORY PASSWORD CHANGE (First Login):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Immediately after logging in for the first time, the student is 
redirected to a password creation screen. They must set their own 
private password before the exam starts.

This ensures that even your own staff cannot access a student's 
private exam once it has begun.

THE 5-PART CAREER ASSESSMENT:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The student is guided through five sections, one at a time:

  SECTION A — Personal Information
  The student confirms their name, class/grade, school name, and 
  contact details.

  SECTION B — Interests & Hobbies
  The student selects from three grids:
    • Activities they enjoy (e.g., "Drawing, painting or designing")
    • Favourite Academic Subjects (e.g., "Mathematics", "Biology")
    • Professions they find interesting (e.g., "Doctor / Nurse")

  SECTION C — Aptitude Self-Assessment (Rating 1 to 5)
  The student rates themselves on 9 key skills from 1 (Not Confident) 
  to 5 (Very Confident). Skills include logical thinking, writing, 
  computer skills, leadership, creativity, and more.

  SECTION D — Personality & Values
  The student selects statements that best describe their character 
  (e.g., "I like leading and taking responsibility").

  SECTION E — Career Vision (Written Answers)
  The student writes short answers about:
    • What careers are they currently considering?
    • Where do they see themselves in 5 years?
    • What does their ideal future life look like?

THE AUTO-SAVE "NEVER LOSE WORK" SYSTEM:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Every few seconds, the website silently saves the student's 
progress in the background. This means:

  • If their internet drops — their answers are safe.
  • If they accidentally close the browser — their answers are safe.
  • If their laptop battery dies — their answers are safe.

When they log back in, they are automatically returned to exactly 
where they stopped. They do not lose a single answer.

AFTER SUBMITTING THE EXAM:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Once the student clicks "Submit" on the final section:
  1. Their completed assessment is saved to your database.
  2. Their temporary login credentials are automatically deleted 
     for security. They cannot log back in with the same password.
  3. Their exam appears in your Admin panel under 
     "Pending AI Analysis" — ready for you to run the AI report.

[PAGE 15]           SAFETY, BACKUPS & DATA PROTECTION
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Your platform has been engineered with enterprise-level protections 
to ensure your business data is never at risk.

DATA BACKUP SYSTEM:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The server runs an automated script (db_backup.sh) that creates 
a complete copy of your entire database on a regular schedule. 
If anything goes wrong at the server level, your student records, 
appointments, and admissions can be fully restored from these copies.

THE TRASH BIN (Accident Recovery):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
If you accidentally delete a student assessment, it does NOT 
permanently disappear. Instead:
  1. The deleted assessment moves to the "Trash Bin" folder.
  2. You can go to: Assessments → Trash Bin.
  3. Click "Restore" to fully recover the assessment.
  4. Only when you click "Permanently Delete" or "Empty Trash" 
     does the record get removed forever.

AUTOMATED SELF-CLEANING (Keeping the System Fast):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The system automatically removes old data to keep the website 
running fast. This happens silently in the background:
  • Appointments older than 90 days are auto-deleted.
  • Assessments older than 90 days are auto-deleted.
  • Temporary student client accounts that were created 3 hours 
    ago but never used are automatically removed every 15 minutes.

HACKER PROTECTION:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  • Login Rate Limiting: Only 5 login attempts per minute are 
    allowed. Anyone trying to guess your password repeatedly 
    is automatically blocked.
  • Session Security: All login sessions expire after 1 hour of 
    inactivity. If you forget to log out, no one else can access 
    your account from the same browser.
  • Password Encryption: All passwords (yours, staff, and students) 
    are stored using Bcrypt — a military-grade encryption method. 
    Even the developers cannot see them.
  • CSRF Protection: Every form on the website has an invisible 
    security token that prevents hackers from submitting fake forms.
  • HTTPS Only: The website forces all traffic through secure 
    HTTPS, meaning all data between you and the website is 
    encrypted while in transit.

TROUBLESHOOTING:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  Q: A student says they cannot log in.
  A: Their temporary password expires after 3 hours if unused. 
     Go to Assessments > Create Client and generate new credentials.

  Q: The AI analysis says "API Error" or "Retry Required."
  A: Go to Gemini AI Settings and check that your API key is 
     correctly saved and not expired.

  Q: The website is showing offline or loading slowly.
  A: This can occasionally happen with AWS EC2 as part of the 
     system's protection cycle. Wait a few minutes and try again. 
     If it continues, contact your developer.

  Q: I deleted a student's assessment by mistake.
  A: Go to Assessments > Trash Bin and click "Restore" on that 
     student's record.

  Q: A parent says their SkillNest application was not received.
  A: Go to SkillNest and check if the application appears there. 
     If admissions were closed at the time, the application would 
     not have gone through and you should ask them to reapply.
